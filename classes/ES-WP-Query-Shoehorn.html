class-es-wp-query-shoehorn.php : 116

Add an 'es' query var to WP_Query which offers seamless integration.

It's worth noting the bug in https://core.trac.wordpress.org/ticket/21169, as it could potentially play a role here. Each hook removes itself, and if it were the only action or filter at that priority, and there was another at a later priority (e.g. 1001), that one wouldn't fire.
